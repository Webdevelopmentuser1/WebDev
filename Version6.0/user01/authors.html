Hall of fame Mangaka

Although there are many great Mangaka that have or will create a manga, here is my list for the top 5 mangaka ever to exist in my opinion

  1. Echiro Oda:creator of One Piece
  2. Tite Kubo: creator of bleach
  3. Makoto Yukimura: creator of Vinland saga
  4. Gege Akutami: creator of Jujutsu Kaisen
  5. Tatsuki Fujimoto: creator of Chainsaw Man
